## Description
FitMatch is a personalized trainer‑matching platform designed to help adults find certified fitness professionals who align with their goals, schedule, and coaching preferences. The system supports customers seeking structured fitness or rehabilitation guidance, as well as providers (trainers and wellness coaches) who want to offer tailored training services. FitMatch streamlines the process of discovering trainers, booking sessions, tracking progress, and exchanging feedback, all within a clean, user‑centered interface.

## App Functions
1. Customer:
    1. **Create/modify customer profile** - Register as a customer and define fitness goals, constraints, and preferences.
    2. **View available services** - Browse trainers by specialization (strength, rehab, weight loss, etc.), certifications, and availability.
    3. **Subscribe to available services** - Book one‑time or recurring training sessions with selected providers.
    4. **Write reviews for subscribed services** - Leave feedback on training quality, communication, and progress.
2. Provider (Trainer):
    1. **Create/modify/remove provider profile** - Register as a trainer and showcase certifications, experience, and coaching style.
    2. **Create services** - Publish training offerings, session types, and availability windows.
    3. **View customer statistics** -  View client goals, session history, and engagement trends.
    4. **Reply to reviews** - Respond professionally to customer feedback.
3. SysAdmin:
    1. **Manage user access** - Approve, suspend, or reinstate customer and provider accounts.
    2. **Moderate services** - Review and remove inappropriate or misleading service listings.
    3. **Moderate reviews** - Flag or remove harmful, fraudulent, or policy‑violating reviews.
    4. **View usage statistics** - Monitor platform activity, booking volume, and user engagement.
